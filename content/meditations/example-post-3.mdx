Welcome to your meditation blog! This post will help you get started with creating great content.

## Creating New Posts

To create a new meditation post:

1. Create a new `.mdx` file in `/content/meditations/`
2. Use kebab-case for filenames: `my-awesome-post.mdx`
3. Add frontmatter at the top (see example above)
4. Write your content in Markdown below the frontmatter
5. Save and refresh - it appears automatically!

## Organizing Your Content

### Using Tags

Tags help readers find related content. Keep them:
- **Consistent**: Use the same tags across posts
- **Lowercase**: Better for URLs and consistency
- **Specific**: "web-development" not just "dev"
- **Limited**: 2-5 tags per post

### Using Popularity (Optional)

The `popularity` field lets you manually curate which posts appear first when readers sort by popularity:

- Set higher numbers (90-100) for your best work
- Medium numbers (60-80) for solid posts
- Lower numbers (30-50) for older or niche content
- Omit entirely if you prefer chronological sorting only

## Writing Tips

### Hook Readers with Strong Openings

Your first paragraph should:
- Grab attention with a question, story, or bold statement
- Preview what readers will learn or gain
- Set expectations for the post's length and depth

### Make It Scannable

- Use descriptive headings
- Break up walls of text
- Include lists and examples
- Add visual breaks with quotes or code blocks

### End with Impact

Your conclusion should:
- Recap key takeaways
- Suggest next steps or further reading
- Invite engagement (though you have a comment system!)

## Content Ideas

Stuck on what to write? Try:

- **Personal experiences**: What have you learned recently?
- **Tutorials**: Teach something you wish you'd known earlier
- **Reviews**: Books, tools, frameworks you've tried
- **Reflections**: Thoughts on industry trends or current events
- **Deep dives**: Explore a topic you're passionate about
- **Case studies**: Document a project from start to finish

## Pro Tips

- **Write regularly**: Consistency matters more than perfection
- **Edit ruthlessly**: Your first draft is just the beginning
- **Get feedback**: Share drafts with trusted friends
- **Update old posts**: Keep evergreen content current
- **Link internally**: Connect related posts together
