## Different Content Structures

### Personal Reflection

Share a personal story or experience. Example structure:
- **Context**: Set the scene
- **Challenge**: What problem or question arose?
- **Insight**: What did you learn or realize?
- **Application**: How does this apply more broadly?

### Tutorial or Guide

Teaching something? Try this structure:
- **Introduction**: What will readers learn?
- **Prerequisites**: What do they need to know first?
- **Step-by-step**: Break it down into clear steps
- **Conclusion**: Recap and next steps

### Opinion or Analysis

Sharing your perspective? Consider:
- **Thesis**: State your main argument
- **Evidence**: Support with examples and reasoning
- **Counter-arguments**: Address alternative views
- **Conclusion**: Synthesize your points
